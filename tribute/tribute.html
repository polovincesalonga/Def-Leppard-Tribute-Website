Facts About Def Leppard

  • Def Leppard is an English band originating from Sheffield. Joe Elliot is the lead vocalist
  • In January 1978, a guitarist known as Steve Clark joined the band. He would be later known as the riff master for coming up with catchy tunes.
  • A member of the band, Tony Kenning, left in the year of 1978. He was replaced by Rick Allend and he became known as the Thunder God,
  • Def Leppard's first concert was in 1978 in a high school gymnasium. Only 6 people became audiences.
  • Phil Collen was a guitarist who replaced Pete Willis in 1982. Pete Willis was released from the band due to excessive drug usage.
  • Phil Collen and Steve Clark became known as the "Terror Twins" because of their close relationship
  • Two famous Def Leppard album sold is called Pyromania(1983) and Hysteria(1987). Both Albums have more than 10 million copies.
  • The song "Photograph" in the album Pyromania is their most popular song to date
